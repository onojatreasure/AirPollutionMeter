Explore Your Air
Explore the air quality anywhere in the world

APM (Air Pollution Meter) is one of the world’s best international air pollution app, which offers anyone free access to the world’s largest air quality database, spanning major locations globally, a list which is growing every day.

This app lets you track your air quality anywhere you are in the world.

Important Notes
  • AQI means Air Quality Index
  • If you are not in a station, values from the nearest station will be shown.
  • Stations around the world have update cycles that are all different from each other.
  • Stations are updated only once per hour.
  • The app shows US AQI
  • If a station doesn’t return pollution values, it means the data wasn’t published for that particular hour.
